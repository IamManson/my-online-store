ABOUT US


We are a bicycle shop in Durbanville that was started in 2015.

It is run by co-owners John and Jane Doe. Our team are passionate about all things cycling and bringing you the best service.

We stock Titan Racing Bikes, Cannondale and SCOTT bikes as well as a wide range of accessories. We have a fantastic workshop to give your wheels the TLC it deserves. We offer basic, minor and major services as well as suspension services and wheel builds.


John Doe | Co-Owner

"My dad has many bicycles around the house when I was growing up and I just fell in love with them from a young age. Cycling gave me a freedom like no other and that's why Jane and I decided to take our love of cycling and open the shop."

Jane Doe | Co-Owner
